About the Site

The purpose of this site is to archive and organize all the photos I've taken since I first received my Canon t3i. Throughout the years, I've learned more about the storytelling capabilities pictures have, and every photo here translates to a special moment dear to me. You'll find collections ranging from specific trips to random photoshoots. My recent collections will always be highlighted on the front page. Enjoy!

About Me

I'm currently a Sophomore at the University of Georgia. I am an intended Entertainment and Media Studies major with a pre-law track. I've been pursuing photography for four years now, with film cinematography as a hobby for three years. I also write articles on Medium, pieces ranging from literature to gaming.
